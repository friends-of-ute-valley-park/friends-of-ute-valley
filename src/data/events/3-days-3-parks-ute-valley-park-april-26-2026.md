The 3 days / 3 parks event is a multiday, multi-park collaboration between the Friends of Peregrine Parks and Open Spaces, the Guardians of Palmer Park, and the Friends of Ute Valley. The idea is to help create a community between the groups and get some awesome work accomplished at Blodgett Open Space, Palmer Park, and Ute Valley Park!

Sunday, April 26th is the Ute Valley Park 3 Days, 3 Parks volunteer day. We will be focusing our efforts on the Vindicator Meadow this year. Volunteer tasks will include picking up trash throughout the meadow, removing noxious weeds, and restoring and seeding social trails and impacted areas around the meadow.

Please wear pants and closed-toe shoes, bring work gloves, sunscreen, water, and snacks.

**Meet up location is at Eagleview Middle School.**

(Note: Due to limited parking Eagleview Middle School has graciously allowed us to utilize their parking lot for the day)

You must register to participate in this event.

All minors participating in the workday must turn in a hard copy of the liability waiver filled out with parental signature. Liability waiver will be available at event sign-in or is accessible at the following address:

https://coloradosprings.gov/parks/page/parks-volunteer-opportunities?mlid=4431

Click "View Consent and Release Form" at the bottom of the page.
